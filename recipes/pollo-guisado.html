Pollo Guisado

A delicious and easy Spanish chicken stew. Great for entertaining and makes for great left overs. Serve over white rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 whole chicken, cut up
  • salt and ground pepper to taste
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup sofrito
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 (8ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 bay lead

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil over medium-high heat in a dutch oven. Season chicken with salt and peper and add to the hot pot to brown.
  2. Reduce heat to medium; add onion to the pot and saute until translucent. Add garlic and wet ingredients.
  3. bring to a boil, reduce heat to low and simmer until chicken falls off bone.
