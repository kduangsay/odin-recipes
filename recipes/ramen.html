Upgraded Instant Ramen

Instant rammen noodles spiced up with veggies and a soft boiled egg.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 packs chicken flavored instant ramen

Directions

  1. Set aside noodles and combine all other ingredients in a large pot. Bring to a boil and add noodles. Cook until noodles are tender yet firm to the bite.
  2. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook until vegetables are tender, about 10 minutes.
